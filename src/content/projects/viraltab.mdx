# ViralTab News | Freelance Journalism Platform

## **Overview**

ViralTab News is a news website founded in May 2016, specialized in freelance journalism and international media distribution. The platform provides articles, images, videos, and other editorial materials, covering viral topics, war, media, promotions, and more.

## Technologies & Architecture

- **Drupal 3 & Drupal 7**: The platform was developed using Drupal versions, adapted for media and editorial content management.
- **Editorial distribution**: Integration with agencies, collaborators, and freelance journalists.
- **Security & rights**: Strict policies regarding copyright, licensing, and content usage.

## Main Features

- Publishing and distribution of articles, images, videos, and multimedia materials
- Collaboration with freelance journalists and media agencies
- International and regional coverage
- Clear policies for copyright, licensing, and editorial usage
- Platform adapted for media partners and end users
